Frequently Asked Questions

What is Marine Minds? ▼
Marine Minds is an interactive web-based game designed to enhance ocean literacy by providing engaging gameplay and educational content about ocean conservation.
How can I play the game? ▼
You can play the game directly on our website through any modern web browser. No downloads or installations are required.
Is Marine Minds free to use? ▼
Yes, Marine Minds is free to use. We also offer premium features and content through our freemium model to enhance the gaming experience.
How do I contact support? ▼
For support, you can contact us through our website's contact form or email us at support@marineminds.com.
